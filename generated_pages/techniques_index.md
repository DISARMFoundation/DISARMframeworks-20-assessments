<td><a href="techniques/A6001.md">A6001</a></td>
<td>US DOJ Malign Foreign Influence Campaign Cycle Assessment</td>
<td>In 2018, the US Department of Justice introduced the Malign Foreign Influence Campaign Cycle (MFICC) as part of its [Cyber-Digital Task Force report](https://web.archive.org/web/20240616065252/https://media-cldnry.s-nbcnews.com/image/upload/today/z_creative/Cyber-Digital%20Task%20Force%20Report.pdf); a framework mapping the phases of a foreign influence operation in order to identify opportunities for detection and disruption at each stage, and can be seen as analogous to a Kill Chain model. While broader in scope, it has a particular emphasis on operations targeting US elections and democratic institutions.</td>
<td>TA90</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><a href="techniques/A6001.001.md">A6001.001</a></td>
<td>Research</td>
<td>Activity has been reviewed against the [US DOJ's Malign Foreign Influence Campaign Cycle](https://web.archive.org/web/20240616065252/https://media-cldnry.s-nbcnews.com/image/upload/today/z_creative/Cyber-Digital%20Task%20Force%20Report.pdf) and assessed to be in the Research stage.<br><br>During the Research stage, the actor identifies the vulnerabilities (both social and technical) it intends to exploit. This involves collection and analysis activities carried out by government research institutes and intelligence services, with the objective of mapping existing social and political fissures within the target society and developing message themes designed to inflame them. </td>
<td>TA90</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><a href="techniques/A6001.002.md">A6001.002</a></td>
<td>Position</td>
<td>Activity has been reviewed against the [US DOJ's Malign Foreign Influence Campaign Cycle](https://web.archive.org/web/20240616065252/https://media-cldnry.s-nbcnews.com/image/upload/today/z_creative/Cyber-Digital%20Task%20Force%20Report.pdf) and assessed to be in the Position stage.<br><br>During the Position stage, the actor establishes the infrastructure and online presence needed to conduct the campaign. Intelligence services, contractors, agents, and assets work to procure technical infrastructure, develop and grow networks of accounts and personas, and establish the credibility of those personas.</td>
<td>TA90</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><a href="techniques/A6001.003.md">A6001.003</a></td>
<td>Tasking</td>
<td>Activity has been reviewed against the [US DOJ's Malign Foreign Influence Campaign Cycle](https://web.archive.org/web/20240616065252/https://media-cldnry.s-nbcnews.com/image/upload/today/z_creative/Cyber-Digital%20Task%20Force%20Report.pdf) and assessed to be in the Tasking stage.<br><br>During the Tasking stage, the actor translates the research and analytical work of the previous stage into operational direction. Drawing on the message themes and targeting analysis developed during Research, leadership and intelligence services assign specific tasks. This stage acts as the bridge between strategic intent and operational activity, converting an understanding of the target society's vulnerabilities into concrete instructions the activity that follows.</td>
<td>TA90</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><a href="techniques/A6001.004.md">A6001.004</a></td>
<td>Produce</td>
<td>Activity has been reviewed against the [US DOJ's Malign Foreign Influence Campaign Cycle](https://web.archive.org/web/20240616065252/https://media-cldnry.s-nbcnews.com/image/upload/today/z_creative/Cyber-Digital%20Task%20Force%20Report.pdf) and assessed to be in the Produce stage.<br><br>During the Produce stage, the actor creates or obtains content. This can involve hacking to acquire genuine material such as the theft of emails from target organisations), or the composition of fabricated or forged content. Intelligence services, contractors, state media, and other assets can play a role here.</td>
<td>TA90</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><a href="techniques/A6001.005.md">A6001.005</a></td>
<td>Publish</td>
<td>Activity has been reviewed against the [US DOJ's Malign Foreign Influence Campaign Cycle](https://web.archive.org/web/20240616065252/https://media-cldnry.s-nbcnews.com/image/upload/today/z_creative/Cyber-Digital%20Task%20Force%20Report.pdf) and assessed to be in the Publish stage.<br><br>During the Publish stage, the actor introduces content into the broader media environment while deliberately obscuring its true origin. This is achieved through publication via online proxies and personas, and dissemination through the networks established in the Position stage.</td>
<td>TA90</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><a href="techniques/A6001.006.md">A6001.006</a></td>
<td>Amplify</td>
<td>Activity has been reviewed against the [US DOJ's Malign Foreign Influence Campaign Cycle](https://web.archive.org/web/20240616065252/https://media-cldnry.s-nbcnews.com/image/upload/today/z_creative/Cyber-Digital%20Task%20Force%20Report.pdf) and assessed to be in the Amplify stage.<br><br>During the Amplify stage, the actor attempts to saturate the information environment and generate genuine public confusion. Bots and ‘trolls’ can artificially inflate the reach and apparent legitimacy of content, while state media and contractors push overt messaging. Unwitting audiences can also contribute as actors at this stage, amplifying foreign narratives without any awareness that they are doing so (for instance by sharing content or attending rallies organised covertly by foreign actors).</td>
<td>TA90</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><a href="techniques/A6001.007.md">A6001.007</a></td>
<td>Calibrate</td>
<td>Activity has been reviewed against the [US DOJ's Malign Foreign Influence Campaign Cycle](https://web.archive.org/web/20240616065252/https://media-cldnry.s-nbcnews.com/image/upload/today/z_creative/Cyber-Digital%20Task%20Force%20Report.pdf) and assessed to be in the Calibrate stage.<br><br>During the Calibrate stage, the actor gathers feedback and adapts to countermeasures deployed against them during the campaign; leadership and intelligence services assess the outcome of the campaign and retool their approach if needed. This emphasises the “Cycle” element of the Malign Foreign Influence Campaign Cycle; a foreign influence operation is not a linear, one-time effort but an iterative process that evolves in response to what is working.</td>
<td>TA90</td>
</tr>
