# Assessment A6001: US DOJ Malign Foreign Influence Campaign Cycle Assessment

**Summary**: In 2018, the US Department of Justice introduced the Malign Foreign Influence Campaign Cycle (MFICC) as part of its [Cyber-Digital Task Force report](https://web.archive.org/web/20240616065252/https://media-cldnry.s-nbcnews.com/image/upload/today/z_creative/Cyber-Digital%20Task%20Force%20Report.pdf); a framework mapping the phases of a foreign influence operation in order to identify opportunities for detection and disruption at each stage, and can be seen as analogous to a Kill Chain model. While broader in scope, it has a particular emphasis on operations targeting US elections and democratic institutions.
