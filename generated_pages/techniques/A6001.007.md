# Technique A6001.007: Calibrate

**Summary**: Activity has been reviewed against the [US DOJ's Malign Foreign Influence Campaign Cycle](https://web.archive.org/web/20240616065252/https://media-cldnry.s-nbcnews.com/image/upload/today/z_creative/Cyber-Digital%20Task%20Force%20Report.pdf) and assessed to be in the Calibrate stage.<br><br>During the Calibrate stage, the actor gathers feedback and adapts to countermeasures deployed against them during the campaign; leadership and intelligence services assess the outcome of the campaign and retool their approach if needed. This emphasises the “Cycle” element of the Malign Foreign Influence Campaign Cycle; a foreign influence operation is not a linear, one-time effort but an iterative process that evolves in response to what is working.
