# Assessment A7002.001: Low Quality Non-English Language Moderation

**Summary**: Western platforms have been documented to have [lower quality moderation when operating in non-English languages.](https://globalwitness.org/en/campaigns/digital-threats/how-big-tech-platforms-are-neglecting-their-non-english-language-users/) This can result in erroneous moderation action, or in continuing to host Assets or Content which goes against the platform's stated terms of service.
