# Assessment A7005.002: Echo Chamber

**Summary**: An environment in which participants predominantly encounter information and views that reinforce those they already hold, formed through social self-selection of like-minded sources and communities rather than algorithmic curation. Actors can exploit echo chambers to seed and amplify narratives that face little internal challenge and harden the community against outside correction.
