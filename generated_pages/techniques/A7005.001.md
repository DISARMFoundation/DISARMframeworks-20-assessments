# Assessment A7005.001: Information Vacuum

**Summary**: An absence or scarcity of credible information on a topic within an information environment. Actors can exploit a vacuum by filling it with their own narratives before authoritative information becomes available, and can deliberately create one by suppressing or degrading access to credible sources.
